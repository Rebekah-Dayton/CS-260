Hello!

Thank you for taking the time to look at my work and learn more about me! My name is Rebekah and I am a crafter at heart. Ever since I was little, I've enjoyed creating things! Whether it was a lopsided hat or cookies for my family, I loved being able to take something and make it into something else. Over quarantine, I had the time to teach myself how to crochet with the help of YouTube tutorials. From there, it just spiraled as younger cousins and siblings asked me if I could make something and then I would give it a try. When I'm not crafting, you can find me studying or doing a movie marathon with friends! Once again, thank you for the time you spent here and I hope you enjoyed!

Sincerely,

Rebekah Dayton
